- **Fixed monthly fee.** Quoted after a 10-minute site walk-through.
- **Inclusions written in.** Scope, frequency, areas, exclusions all in the agreement.
- **Consumables and equipment included** unless explicitly excluded.
- **GST shown separately.**
- **No lock-in beyond 3 months.** 30 days notice either side after that.

- Public liability insurance: $10M, current Certificate of Currency on request
- Workers compensation: WorkSafe Victoria coverage applies to every cleaner

- Subcontractors engaged on signed written agreement aligning with Cleaning Services Award 2020
- Working with Children Check for any school / childcare placement

- Photo completion report on request

- 48-hour re-clean guarantee if anything is missed
- Direct line to {{your_name}} for any issue — not a call centre

## Why a small specialist instead of a chain

We're a focused Melbourne operator, not a national franchise. That means:

- Real local accountability — you talk to the operator, not a 1300 number
- Specialist sub-network deployed only when the contract calls for it (medical, builders, carpet steam)
- Faster response times — typically same-day for site issues
- Pricing transparency — no margin hidden in opaque "site supervision fees"
